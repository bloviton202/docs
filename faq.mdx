## General

### What is Presti.ai?
AI-powered product image generation for furniture. Transform simple photos into photorealistic lifestyle scenes in seconds.

### How does it work?
1. Upload product photo  
2. Position on canvas  
3. Describe desired scene  
4. AI generates environment  
5. Download result  

### Who uses Presti.ai?
300+ furniture brands, manufacturers, e-commerce retailers, and interior designers.

## Technical

### What formats are supported?
JPG, PNG, WebP.  
Max file size: 50MB  
Min resolution: 1000×1000px

### Product appears floating?
Move the product lower on the canvas to establish contact with the floor.

### Perspective issues?
All products must be shot from the same angle.  
The AI cannot rotate images.

### Which browsers work?
Latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are supported.
