## 1. Start with High-Quality Product Images

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Clean Packshots" icon="camera">
    Use well-lit images with neutral backgrounds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Full Visibility" icon="eye">
    Ensure your product is fully visible and centered.
  </Card>
  <Card title="High Resolution" icon="image">
    Avoid overly cropped or low-resolution images.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## 2. Write Clear, Descriptive Prompts

<Card title="Be Specific" icon="edit">
  Include details about the scene, style, ambiance, and props.
</Card>

<Info>
  **Example:**
  ✅ "Scandinavian living room with light wood floors and large bay windows, soft morning light"

  <br />

  ❌ "Modern room"
</Info>

## 10. Avoid Common Pitfalls

<Warning>
  **To avoid:**

  - Vague prompts (e.g., "nice room", "lamp", "plants").
  - Adding accessories that extend beyond the product’s cutout in post-generation.
  - Skipping the brush sizing step (brush size = item scale).
</Warning>
