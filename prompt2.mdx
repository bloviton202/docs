## 1Ô∏è‚É£ General Furniture

<Card title="Two Approaches" icon="palette">
  Choose between **immersive brand-aligned scenes** or **minimalist clean backgrounds**
</Card>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Brand-Guideline Prompt">
    <Card title="Goal" icon="bullseye">
      Create an immersive scene aligned with brand guidelines
    </Card>
    <Accordion title="Prompt Structure">
      <AccordionGroup>
        <Accordion title="Main Subject & Setting">
          - **Focal Object:** Name of product (e.g., chaise lounge)
          - **Space Type:** Room/environment (e.g., reading nook)
          - **Mood:** Ambiance (e.g., moody, luxurious)
        </Accordion>
        <Accordion title="Architectural Details">
          - Windows/Doors: Style, materials, details
          - Walls/Floors/Ceilings: Colors, textures, patterns
          - **Color Palette:** Specific names/codes
        </Accordion>
        <Accordion title="Furniture & Decor">
          - Primary/secondary furniture
          - Decorative objects and textiles
        </Accordion>
        <Accordion title="Lighting & Atmosphere">
          - Light source, time of day, effects
          - Desired emotional feeling
        </Accordion>
      </AccordionGroup>
    </Accordion>
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Define Focal Object">
        Example: _"Chaise lounge in a moody traditional reading corner"_
        <Tip>
          Specify room purpose and dominant style
        </Tip>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Describe Architecture">
        Example: _"Tudor-style windows with black diamond-pattern leaded glass"_
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Furniture/Accessories">
        Example: _"White marble side table with black veining"_
      </Step>
      <Step title="Specify Textiles">
        Example: _"Floor-to-ceiling taupe linen drapes"_
      </Step>
      <Step title="Detail Lighting">
        Example: _"Golden hour light casting geometric shadows"_
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Info>
      **Template:**

      ```markdown
      "[Focal object] in a [room] with [mood] ambiance.
      Position near [feature] with [materials/colors].
      Add [furniture] in [material].
      Light with [source], creating [effects].
      Outside: [view]. Atmosphere: [emotion]."
      ```
    </Info>
    <Card title="Luxury Example" icon="gem">
      _"Emerald velvet sofa in Art Deco living room with gold wallpaper,
      black marble floors, and crystal chandelier.
      Parisian balcony view at golden hour."_
    </Card>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Minimalist Prompt">
    <Card title="Goal" icon="focus">
      Isolate product in ultra-simple setting
    </Card>
    <Check>
      High-resolution product image
    </Check>
    <Check>
      Neutral background (e.g., matte white)
    </Check>
    <Check>
      Even lighting without shadows
    </Check>
    <Info>
      **Template:**

      ```markdown
      "[Product], [color/material], [angle].
      On [floor], against [wall].
      Lighting: [type], even and shadow-free."
      ```
    </Info>
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Sofa Example" icon="couch">
        _"White ceramic vase, 3/4 angle,
        on light oak floor, beige wall.
        Diffused natural light."_
      </Card>
      <Card title="Chair Example" icon="chair">
        _"Black leather chair, side view,
        on gray concrete, light gray wall.
        Neutral studio lighting."_
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Lamps & Accessories

<Card title="Two Approaches" icon="lightbulb">
  **Zoomed-in** for detail focus or **zoomed-out** for context
</Card>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Zoomed-In">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Define Product">
        Example: _"Brass lamp with linen shade on textured linen"_
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Backdrop">
        Example: _"Rich tapestry in muted greens/browns"_
      </Step>
      <Step title="Supporting Elements">
        Example: _"Ceramic vase with dried foliage"_
      </Step>
      <Step title="Lighting">
        Example: _"Warm ambient glow with subtle shadows"_
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Info>
      **Template:**

      ```markdown
      "[Lamp], [materials/colors],
      on [surface] with [backdrop].
      Nearby: [decor]. Lighting: [effects].
      Mood: [cozy/elegant]."
      ```
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Zoomed-Out">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Define Product">
        Example: _"Modern chandelier in dining room"_
      </Step>
      <Step title="Room Setting">
        Example: _"Grey plaster walls, concrete floor"_
      </Step>
      <Step title="Furniture/Decor">
        Example: _"Dark wood door, white rug, geometric sculptures"_
      </Step>
      <Step title="Lighting">
        Example: _"Sunlight \+ chandelier glow"_
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Info>
      **Template:**

      ```markdown
      "[Lamp] in [room] with [walls/floors].
      Furniture: [pieces]. Decor: [elements].
      Lighting: [source] creating [ambiance]."
      ```
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  **Zoomed-in** for social media/detail shots.
  **Zoomed-out** for interior design catalogs.
</Warning>
