## Critical Canvas Placement Rules

### The Permanent Position Principle

> **CRITICAL:** Where you place the product on canvas is its **FINAL** position. This cannot be changed after generation.

#### Placement Steps

1. **Upload Product** â€“ Import your silo or lifestyle shot  
2. **Position Carefully** â€“ Place product where it should appear in final scene  
3. **Scale Appropriately** â€“ Size tells AI about floor level and perspective  
4. **Leave Space** â€“ Empty canvas space = room for AI to build scene

### Common Positioning Mistakes

| **Mistake**            | **Result**              | **Solution**                  |
|------------------------|-------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Product fills canvas   | No room for scene       | Scale down, reposition        |
| Floating placement     | Unrealistic scenes      | Lower to establish floor      |
| Edge placement         | Cropped results         | Center with margin            |
| Wrong scale            | Proportion issues       | Reference real-world size     |

## Photography Best Practices

### Lighting Setup

- Even lighting, minimize harsh shadows  
- Neutral color to avoid color casts  
- Shoot from multiple angles  
- Keep exposure consistent

### Product Preparation

- Clean thoroughly â€“ dust and marks show  
- Steam or iron fabrics  
- Check alignment and symmetry  
- Stage naturally

### Technical Tips

#### Camera Settings
- Use a tripod
- ISO below 800
- Aperture around f/8
- Shoot in RAW if possible

#### Post-Processing
- Remove background if needed
- Adjust brightness/contrast
- Avoid heavy filters

#### File Handling
- Export high-res JPEG or PNG
- Keep original version
- Avoid recompression
