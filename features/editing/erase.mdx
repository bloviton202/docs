## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the Tool">
    Choose the **Magic Erase** tool from the editing panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paint Over Objects">
    Use the brush to mark the elements you want to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Processing">
    The AI fills the area seamlessly, preserving lighting and scene consistency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **For best results:**
  - Paint slightly beyond object edges for better blending.
  - Work on one object at a time.
</Tip>
