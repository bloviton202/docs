### When to use Resize Product

Use **Resize Product** when you want to:

- Increase or reduce your productâ€™s prominence in the scene
- Correct scale proportions between elements
- Fine-tune product positioning
- Remove unwanted overlaps or fix perspective issues

---

### How it works

1. Open the **Edit Panel**
2. Select **Resize Product**
3. Adjust the bounding box to reposition or rescale the product
4. Validate to regenerate the scene with the updated placement
