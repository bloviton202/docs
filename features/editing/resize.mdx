## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Edit Panel">
    Access the editing tools for your generated scene.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Resize Product">
    Choose the **Resize Product** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust Bounding Box">
    Drag and resize the bounding box to reposition or rescale your product.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate Changes">
    Confirm to regenerate the scene with the updated placement.
  </Step>
</Steps>
