## When to Use Replace Product

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Test Product Variants" icon="git-compare">
    Try different versions of your product in the same scene.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Create Family Shots" icon="image">
    Maintain consistency across a product line.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Run A/B Tests" icon="activity">
    Compare different products with identical backgrounds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Preserve Scene Details" icon="copy">
    Keep the same background, lighting, and shadows.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Important Requirements

<Warning>
  The **new product image must be shot from the same angle and perspective** as the original.
</Warning>

<Info>
  Since the background and scene remain unchanged, matching angles ensure a natural, seamless integration.
</Info>

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the Tool">
    Open the editing panel and choose **Replace Product**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload New Product">
    Add your new product image, ensuring it matches the original angle and perspective.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Processing">
    The AI swaps the product while preserving the background, lighting, and shadows.
  </Step>
</Steps>
