## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the Object">
    Click on the element you want to replace in the scene.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the Replacement">
    Type a **precise description** of what should appear instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Transformation">
    The AI swaps the element while preserving lighting, perspective, and context.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The AI maintains the original shape and placement. **Be specific** in your description for the best results.
  <br />
  **Example:**
  - ✅ "Replace the wooden table with a black marble table"
  - ❌ "Change the table"
</Tip>
