## Lighting Options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Low">
    <Card title="Dim, Moody Atmosphere" icon="moon">
      Ideal for creating ambiance and depth.
    </Card>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Medium">
    <Card title="Balanced Daylight" icon="sunrise">
      Soft shadows, perfect for e-commerce.
    </Card>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="High">
    <Card title="Bright, Clean Look" icon="sun">
      Minimal shadows, great for product showcases.
    </Card>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  **Recommendations:**

  - **Medium** → Best for e-commerce
  - **Low** → Great for ambiance shots
  - **High** → Perfect for clean product showcases
</Tip>

<Info>
  Each lighting option generates **4 variations**. Pick your favorite or regenerate until you’re satisfied.
</Info>

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Lighting Option">
    Choose between **Low**, **Medium**, or **High** lighting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview Variations">
    Review the 4 generated variations for each option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Favorite">
    Select the one that best fits your needs or regenerate for more options.
  </Step>
</Steps>
