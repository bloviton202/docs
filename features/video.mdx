## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or Select a Still Image">
    <Card title="Base Image" icon="image">
      Start with your favorite generated lifestyle scene as your base image.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a Motion Prompt">
    <Card title="Describe the Action" icon="edit">
      Click the video icon and describe the action or movement you want in the scene.
    </Card>
    <CodeGroup>

    ```text
    "Golden retriever walks across the room from left to right"
    ```

    
    ```text
    "Person enters and sits on the chair"
    ```

    
    ```text
    "Coffee cup steams while person picks it up"
    ```

    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Processes the Video">
    <Info>
      Smooth camera movements and subtle animations are generated in about 1 minute.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Your Video">
    <Card title="Export" icon="download">
      Export as an MP4 file, ready to use anywhere.
    </Card>
  </Step>
</Steps>
