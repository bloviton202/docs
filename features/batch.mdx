## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Products">
    <Card title="Add Images" icon="upload">
      Upload all product images you want to process in one batch.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Your Prompt">
    <Card title="Describe the Scene" icon="edit">
      Define the scene, style, or vibe to apply to all products.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Format">
    <Card title="Choose Aspect Ratio" icon="crop">
      Pick the format best suited for your use (e.g., Instagram post, website banner).
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    <Card title="Start Batch" icon="play">
      Presti creates individually styled images for each product, matching your prompt’s look and feel.
    </Card>
  </Step>
</Steps>
