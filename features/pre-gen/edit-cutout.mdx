---
title: "Edit Cutout"
description: "Perfect your product image before generating a background."
---
### When to Use This Tool
Use **Edit Cutout** whenever you need to:
- Fix imperfections after automatic background removal
- Restore small product details that were accidentally erased
- Remove unwanted objects still visible in the cutout

---
### How to Access and Edit Your Cutout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Tool">
    Find **Edit Cutout** below your product in the initial canvas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Editing Method">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Brush Tool">
        - Paint over areas to **erase** or **restore**
        - Ideal for quick fixes or larger areas
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Guided Cutout">
        - Place points around the area to **keep** or **remove**
        - Creates **cleaner, sharper edges**
        - Perfect for fine details or complex shapes
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
</Steps>
