## When to Use This Tool

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Fix Imperfections" icon="check">
    After automatic background removal, correct any errors or missed spots.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Restore Details" icon="restore">
    Bring back small product details that were accidentally erased.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Remove Unwanted Objects" icon="trash">
    Clean up any remaining unwanted elements in the cutout.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## How to Access and Edit Your Cutout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Tool">
    Find **Edit Cutout** below your product in the initial canvas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Editing Method">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Brush Tool">
        <Card title="Quick Fixes" icon="brush">
          - Paint over areas to **erase** or **restore**.
          - Adjust brush size for precision.
          - Ideal for larger areas or simple corrections.
        </Card>
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Guided Cutout">
        <Card title="Precision Editing" icon="scissors">
          - Place points around the area to **keep** or **remove**.
          - Creates **cleaner, sharper edges**.
          - Perfect for fine details or complex shapes.
        </Card>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
</Steps>
