## Why Choose Presti?

### Performance and Speed

Generate photorealistic lifestyle scenes in seconds, not days. Our AI model is specifically trained on furniture to ensure material consistency, realistic shadows, and accurate scaling.\
Each generation takes only seconds, allowing you to iterate quickly and perfect your image in 5-10 minutes using our editing tools.

### Cost Efficiency

Reduce your visual content costs by up to 90% compared to traditional photoshoots and 3D renders.\
No more expenses for:

- Studio rental and setup
- Professional photography equipment
- Styling and staging costs
- Post-production workflows
- Logistics and transportation

### Built Exclusively for Furniture

Presti isn’t a generic tool — it’s purpose-built for furniture.\
Our AI is trained on a vast, dedicated dataset, mastering your industry’s specific challenges:

- Materials: wood, fabric, leather, metal — perfectly rendered every time
- Physics: realistic shadows and weight that make furniture look real
- Scale: perfect proportions in any room setting
- Quality: high-resolution, photorealistic results indistinguishable from real photos
- Standards: e-commerce ready, professional results

### Measurable Business Impact

- 300\+ furniture brands actively using Presti
- 10x faster time-to-market for new product launches
- 90% reduction in visual content production costs
- 85% decrease in product photography turnaround time
- 3x increase in visual asset variety per SKU
