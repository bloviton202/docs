## Content Removal & Replacement

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Magic Erase">
    <Card title="Clean Up Your Scene" icon="eraser">
      **Purpose:** Remove unwanted elements from the generated scene with a simple brush tool.

      **Perfect For:**

      - Removing distracting objects
      - Cleaning up artifacts
      - Simplifying busy compositions
      - Creating cleaner backgrounds
    </Card>
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Tool">
        Choose Magic Erase from the editing toolbar
      </Step>
      <Step title="Brush Over">
        Paint over unwanted elements with the brush
      </Step>
      <Step title="AI Processing">
        AI intelligently removes and fills the selected areas
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    Learn more about Magic Erase →
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Magic Replace">
    <Card title="Transform Elements" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
      **Purpose:** Replace specific parts of your image with new elements using a targeted prompt.

      **Capabilities:**

      - Replace furniture pieces
      - Change wall colors or textures
      - Swap decorative elements
      - Transform architectural features
    </Card>
    <Warning>
      Be specific in your replacement prompts for best results
    </Warning>
    Learn more about Magic Replace →
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Scene Enhancement

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Post-Generation Accessorize">
    <Card title="Enrich Your Scene" icon="plus-circle">
      **Purpose:** Add new accessories even after the background has been created (plants, people, objects, etc.).

      **Popular Additions:**

      - Plants and greenery
      - People and lifestyle elements
      - Decorative objects
      - Seasonal items
      - Brand elements
    </Card>
    <Tip>
      Unlike pre-generation accessorizing, this tool adds elements to an existing scene without regenerating the entire background.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Strategic Placement">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Identify Gaps">

      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose Elements">
        Select accessories that complement your product and style
      </Step>
      <Step title="Natural Integration">
        Ensure new elements fit naturally within the existing lighting and perspective
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Format Optimization

<Card title="Aspect Ratio Adjustment" icon="crop">
  **Purpose:** Reframe or adapt your image into a new format without regenerating from scratch.

  <CardGroup cols={2}>
    <Card title="Time Efficient" icon="clock">
      No need to regenerate the entire scene
    </Card>
    <Card title="Multiple Formats" icon="grid">
      Create versions for different platforms
    </Card>
  </CardGroup>
</Card>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Common Ratios">
    | Format    | Ratio | Best For                       |
    | --------- | ----- | ------------------------------ |
    | Square    | 1:1   | Instagram posts, thumbnails    |
    | Portrait  | 4:5   | Instagram stories, Pinterest   |
    | Landscape | 16:9  | Website headers, presentations |
    | Vertical  | 9:16  | TikTok, mobile stories         |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Smart Cropping">
    The tool intelligently:

    - Maintains your product as the focal point
    - Preserves important scene elements
    - Adapts composition for new dimensions
    - Keeps visual balance intact
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
