## Accepted File Formats

<Accordion title="File Format Details">
  <AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="Supported Formats">
      - **.jpg, .png, .webp** (all common image types)
      - Maximum compatibility across platforms
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Transparency Support">
      - **PNG with alpha channel** is ideal for precise cutouts
      - Maintains clean edges and professional appearance
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Size Limitations">
      - Max file size: up to **50MB per image**
      - Compress if needed while maintaining quality
    </Accordion>
  </AccordionGroup>
</Accordion>

## Resolution Recommendations

<Warning>
  **Always upload your highest-resolution photos.** AI can't add details that aren't there.
</Warning>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Minimum">
    **1000×1000 px**

    - Web use only
    - Basic quality output
    - Not recommended for professional use
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Recommended">
    **2000×2000 px**

    - Standard quality results
    - Good for most applications
    - Balanced file size vs quality
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Optimal">
    **4000×4000 px\+**

    - Print quality & zoom capability
    - Maximum detail preservation
    - Best AI processing results
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Photography Best Practices

### Lighting Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Lighting Setup">
    - Use soft, even lighting to avoid harsh shadows
    - Neutral color temperature with no color casts
    - Keep exposure consistent across shots
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Shooting Strategy">
    - Shoot from multiple angles to help AI understand the product's shape
    - Maintain consistent lighting conditions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Product Preparation

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Cleaning">
    - Clean the product thoroughly (no dust or marks)
    - Remove any packaging residue
    - Polish reflective surfaces
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Styling">
    - Steam or iron fabrics to remove wrinkles
    - Check alignment and symmetry
    - Position the product naturally and attractively
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Common Issues & Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🔍 Blurry Images" icon="magnifying-glass">
    **Issue:** Source photo resolution too low

    **Solution:** Use a higher-resolution image from your original shoot
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="🎨 Color Shifts" icon="palette">
    **Issue:** Inconsistent colors across images

    **Solution:** Check your white balance settings and maintain consistent lighting
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="📝 Lost Details" icon="file-lines">
    **Issue:** Fine details not visible in final output

    **Solution:** Provide sharper, higher-quality source images
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
