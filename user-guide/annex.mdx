# Team Collaboration

Presti makes teamwork easy with **shared albums** and a **simple review mode**.

<Info>
  **Available on:** Team and Enterprise plans
</Info>

---

## Shared Albums

<Card title="Organize & Collaborate" icon="folder-open">
  Group your images by project, campaign, or any way that fits your workflow.
</Card>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an album">
    Organize your images by project, client, or theme.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add team members">
    Invite colleagues and set custom permissions for each member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collaborate in real time">
    Team members can view, comment, and work together on shared images.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Review Mode

<Card title="Simplify Image Approvals" icon="check-circle">
  Streamline the feedback and approval process with a dedicated review workflow.
</Card>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="How it works">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Turn on review mode">
        Switch any album to review mode to start the approval process.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Gather feedback">
        Reviewers can leave comments directly on images for clear, contextual feedback.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Track status">
        Each image shows its status: **Pending Review**, **Changes Requested**, or **Approved**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Key Features">
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Comments" icon="comment">
        Provide clear, direct feedback on specific parts of an image.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Status Tracking" icon="list">
        Visual indicators show the approval status of each image at a glance.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

## Tips for Effective Reviews

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Set Clear Expectations" icon="target">
    Define what "approved" means for your team to avoid confusion.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Write Detailed Comments" icon="edit">
    Be specific about what needs to change or what works well.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review in Batches" icon="grid">
    Group similar images to speed up the review process.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
