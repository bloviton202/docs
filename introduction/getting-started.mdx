<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Head over to [presti.ai](http://presti.ai) and create your free account to get started.

    <Info>
      No credit card required for your free trial\!
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Your First Project">
    Once registered, you'll be able to create your first project directly from the dashboard.

    <Tip>
      Choose a descriptive project name to keep your work organized from the start.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload and Generate">
    Simply load an initial silo image from your catalog, enter an initial prompt, and Presti will do the rest\! This is the **starting block** for any project in Presti, where you can bring your lifestyle ideas to life instantly.

    <Warning>
      Make sure your product images have clean, transparent backgrounds for best results.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

## Quick Tips for Success

<Info>
  **Pro Tip:** Start with high-quality product images and clear prompts. The better your input, the more stunning your results will be\!
</Info>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Image Quality Tips" icon="image">
    - Use high-resolution product images (minimum 1000px)
    - Ensure clean, transparent backgrounds
    - Good lighting on the original product shot
    - Multiple angles available for complex products
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Prompt Writing" icon="pen-nib">
    - Be specific about the scene you want
    - Include lighting preferences (natural, warm, dramatic)
    - Mention the target audience or use case
    - Reference style or mood keywords
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Workflow Optimization" icon="workflow">
    - Batch similar products together
    - Create templates for recurring scenes
    - Use consistent naming conventions
    - Organize projects by product categories
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
