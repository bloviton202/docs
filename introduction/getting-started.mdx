### Create Your First Project

1. **Step 1**
   To get started, head over to [presti.ai](http://presti.ai) and create an account.
2. **Step 2**
   Once registered, you’ll be able to create your first project.
3. **Step 3**
   Simply load an initial silo image from your catalog, enter an initial prompt, and Presti will do the rest\! This is the **starting block** for any project in Presti, where you can bring your lifestyle ideas to life instantly.
