## Creating with Presti

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How to make sure to get a perfect result with Presti?">
    **Pro Tips for Flawless Generations**

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="1. Start with High-Quality Images" icon="camera">
        - Use **high-resolution** product photos (minimum 1000px)
        - Shoot from **consistent angles** for multi-product scenes
        - Good lighting on original product shots improves AI interpretation
      </Card>
      <Card title="2. Write Precise and Detailed Prompts" icon="edit">
        - Be **specific** about the scene (e.g., "Modern Scandinavian living room with oak flooring")
        - Include **lighting preferences** (natural/daylight/evening)
        - Mention **target audience** or use case (e.g., "luxury hotel lobby")
        - Add **style references** (minimalist, vintage, industrial)

        <Info>
          Example: ✅ "Sunny breakfast nook with white marble countertop and rattan chairs"

          <br />

          Avoid: ❌ "Nice kitchen"
        </Info>
      </Card>
      <Card title="3. Use Reference Images" icon="image">
        - Upload **mood board images** for style guidance
        - Select from Presti's **curated style library**
      </Card>
      <Card title="4. Leverage Editing Tools" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
        - Use **Relight** to adjust ambiance after generation
        - **Resize/Reposition** products for perfect composition
        - **Magic Erase** to remove unwanted elements
        - **Color Swap** to test different product variations
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
    <Info>
      **Bonus Tip:** Don't hesitate to iterate on your first generations and edits — you always have access to your history and have unlimited generations\!
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What should I do when I hit an error?">
    **Troubleshooting Guide**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="1. Check Image Size">
        If the error occurs when downloading an image, the issue is most likely related to the **image size**: verify that your image **isn't too big** for the upload.
      </Step>
      <Step title="2. Basic Checks">
        If it's not an image size issue:

        - **Refresh the page** (F5 or Ctrl\+R)
        - Check your **internet connection**
        - Verify you're not using a **VPN** (disable it if you are)
        - Try a different **browser** (Chrome/Firefox recommended)
        - Clear your browser **cache and cookies**
      </Step>
      <Step title="3. Contact Support">
        If the problem persists:

        - Describe the error in detail (screenshot if possible)
        - Mention what you were trying to do when it occurred
        - Contact our team via **live chat** for immediate assistance

        <Card title="Need Help?" icon="message-circle" href="/support">
          [Open Live Chat →](#)
        </Card>
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Warning>
      **Pro Tip:** Most download errors (90%) are resolved by checking image size or disabling VPN.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to complete a project?">
    20 minutes max are needed to get a **perfect result**\!
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I find the Editing Tools?">
    You can find every Editing Tools under your image by clicking on the **Edit button**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I rename a product?">
    Yes, you can rename a product when you click on **View Details** of this product in the **Product Library**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I collaborate on my projects?" icon="users">
    **Team Collaboration Features**

    <Info>
      Yes\! **Team collaboration is available** for users on **Team and Enterprise plans**.
    </Info>
    <CardGroup cols={1}>
      <Card title="Multi-User Access" icon="user-plus">
        Invite team members to work together on projects.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Real-Time Comments" icon="message-square">
        Leave feedback directly on images.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Role-Based Permissions" icon="settings">
        Assign different access levels (viewer, editor, admin).
      </Card>
      <Card title="Shared Workspaces" icon="folder">
        Organize projects in shared folders for easy access.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I see my history?" icon="clock">
    **Access Your Generation & Download History**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="1. Go to Album Section">
        Navigate to the **Album** tab in the left sidebar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="2. Apply Filters">
        Use the filter options to refine your search:

        - **Date range** (last 7 days, month, custom)
        - **Product type** (sofa, chair, table, etc.)
        - **Team member** (filter by who created the image)
      </Step>
      <Step title="3. Export or Reuse">
        Once found, you can:

        - **Download** the image again
        - **Reuse** the prompt for new generations
        - **Share** with your team
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Tip>
      Use the **search bar** to quickly find specific projects by name or keyword.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
