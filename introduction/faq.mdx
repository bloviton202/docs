## Getting started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Presti.ai?">
    AI-powered product image generation for furniture. Transform simple photos into photorealistic lifestyle scenes in seconds.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How does it work?">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Upload product photo">

      </Step>
      <Step title="Position on canvas">
        Place your product where you want it
      </Step>
      <Step title="Describe desired scene">
        Enter a prompt for the environment
      </Step>
      <Step title="AI generates environment">
        Let the AI create your scene
      </Step>
      <Step title="Download result">
        Export in your desired resolution
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

    350+ furniture brands, manufacturers, e-commerce retailers, and interior designers.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I subscribe?">

  <Accordion title="Can I start from examples?">

## Managing your account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change the email I use to login?">
    Unfortunately, changing the email address directly is not possible at this time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change the workspace owner?">
    To change the workspace owner, please email **[support@presti.ai](mailto:support@presti.ai)**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I find my invoices?">
    Go to your profile → **Manage subscription** → Click on **Stripe portal**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What email should I use in the Stripe Portal?">
    Use the same email you used to subscribe. If you can't find it, contact us at **[hamza@presti.ai](mailto:hamza@presti.ai)**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

For further questions about credits and subscription issues, see **[Plans and credits](https://docs.presti.ai/plans-and-credits)**.
