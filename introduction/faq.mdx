## Getting started

### What is Presti.ai?
AI-powered product image generation for furniture. Transform simple photos into photorealistic lifestyle scenes in seconds.

### How does it work?
1. Upload product photo
2. Position on canvas
3. Describe desired scene
4. AI generates environment
5. Download result

### Who uses Presti.ai?
350+ furniture brands, manufacturers, e-commerce retailers, and interior designers.

### How do I subscribe?
Directly via the [Pricing page](https://presti.ai/pricing) or contact us by phone or email at **[hamza@presti.ai](mailto:hamza@presti.ai)**.

### Can I start from examples?
Yes! Presti offers a selection of **‘Presti Styles’**, here to help you generate a lifestyle image without having to prompt.

### What should I do when I hit an error?
1. **Try-to-fix button**
   Press the Try-to-fix button. Presti will try to solve the issue automatically. The best part: using the button doesn’t deduct any credits!

2. **Investigate**
   If the Try-to-fix button doesn’t resolve the issue, explain what you are seeing and what specific parts aren’t working.

3. **Restore**
   If that doesn’t work, restore to a previous working version. Clear the AI’s context and try a different approach.

## Managing your account

### Can I change the email I use to login?
Unfortunately, changing the email address directly is not possible at this time.

### Can I change the workspace owner?
To change the workspace owner, please email **[support@presti.ai](mailto:support@presti.ai)**.

### Where can I find my invoices?
Go to your profile → **Manage subscription** → Click on **Stripe portal**.

### What email should I use in the Stripe Portal?
Use the same email you used to subscribe. If you can’t find it, contact us at **[hamza@presti.ai](mailto:hamza@presti.ai)**.
